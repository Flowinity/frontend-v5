(10) Firebase Cloud Messaging for Communications.

TPU Mobile (referred to as PrivateUploader, TPUvNATIVE, or TPU Kotlin), our application for Android devices, utilizes Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to provide instant push notifications to recipients when a message in sent in Communications. FCM is a service provided by Google that enables the delivery of push notifications and other messages to mobile devices.

When you use TPU Mobile and log into your account, certain data may be sent to Google through FCM to enable the delivery of instant notifications. This data may include your device's unique identifier, your notification preferences, and information related to the notifications you receive such as message contents sent by yourself and others in group chats, or direct messages.

Please note that the data sent to Google through FCM is subject to Google Firebase's own privacy policy, this can be found here.

By using TPU Mobile and logging into your account, you acknowledge and consent to the transmission of data to Google for the purpose of providing an optimal experience whilst utilizing TPU's messaging features.

(11) Auto-upload services and programs.

By using auto-upload programs such as ShareX, you agree that all files you have targeted to be automatically uploaded such as screenshots are uploaded to TPU and can be removed at any time via the Gallery, this includes the TPU Mobile uploader which allows you to select one or more directories on your device to monitor and automatically upload all the file contents to the TPU platform for convenience, this can be disabled, and is turned off by default on the installation of TPU Mobile.

(12) Account removal

If you would like your account deleted, please email tpu@troplo.com and we will remove your account and any data associated with it provided you send the request via the verified email account that is currently linked to your TPU account, we may still hold data associated with your account after approval of the deletion request, for example in our regularly scheduled backups.
