Last updated: 2024/01/18

Whilst using Flowinity (or "TroploPrivateUploader" / "PrivateUploader" / "TPU") you agree that you will not:

  1. Upload or create any content that is:
    1. illegal (based on Australian laws).
    2. disturbing.
    3. infringes on the rights of others.
    4. promotion of hate or discrimination, this includes but is not limited to:
      1. race.
      2. sexual orientation.
      3. gender.
      4. age.
    5. deemed offensive.
    6. malicious files (such as viruses, trojans, ransomware) designed to harm users, or act nefariously.
    7. copyright infringing, such as: pirated software, books, movies, TV, etc.
    8. phishing, or other content that is designed to deceive people.
    9. pornography, or other sexual content.
  2. You also must not:
    1. attempt to maliciously interfere with security, or service reliability (example: DoS/DDoS) towards TPU.
    2. attempt to login to another user account without the user's consent (example: brute-forcing a password).
    3. find and exploit a security vulnerability with malicious intent, if you find a security vulnerability or any other issue, please email troplo@troplo.com (thank you!)
    4. have more than 2 TPU accounts, and these accounts must not be used maliciously (example: spam uploads in an attempt to cause service disruption.)
    5. send harmful, or hateful content/messages to other users on TPU.
  3. TPU may at any point, ban, delete, and deactivate your account, or remove any content such as but not limited to, uploads, or documents associated with your account for any reason.

  5. TPU may update this policy, or any others at any point in time.
